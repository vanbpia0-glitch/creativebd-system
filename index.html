Free page audit — 5-point framework
1 — Clarity
Hero message is clear within 5 seconds
Can you tell what they do, who it's for, and what to do next — immediately?
No jargon or vague taglines
"We deliver excellence" = red flag. Specific beats generic always.
2 — Call to action
There is at least one visible CTA above the fold
Button, link, or form — something that tells the visitor what to do.
CTA is specific, not generic
"Book a call" beats "learn more." Action words with outcome.
3 — Content quality
Copy speaks to the customer's problem, not just the brand
Does the page say "we" 10x more than "you"? That's a content issue.
There is some form of proof (testimonial, result, client logo)
Trust signals reduce friction. Missing = lost leads.
4 — SEO basics
Page title and meta description are present and descriptive
Check browser tab + Google preview. Generic = invisible.
H1 exists and includes a relevant keyword
One H1 per page. It should describe what the page is about.
5 — Mobile & speed
Page loads fast on mobile (under 3 seconds)
Open on your phone. If it crawls, it's losing customers.
Layout doesn't break on mobile
Text readable, buttons tappable, no horizontal scroll.
